FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What Services do you offer ?

At Pixelate Nest, we specialize in bringing your digital vision to life. Our core services include custom Web & App Development, professional Video Editing, and strategic Digital Marketing. We also offer on-location Videography to capture high-quality footage for your projects.

How long does it take to complete a project ?

Timelines can vary based on the project's scope. A standard website typically takes between 4 to 8 weeks from start to finish. For video editing, turnaround is usually between 3 to 7 business days, depending on the length and complexity of the footage. We will provide a detailed project timeline in our proposal.

What is your typical project process?

Our process is collaborative and transparent. It starts with a discovery call to understand your vision, followed by a detailed proposal. Once approved, we move to the design and development/editing phase, with regular check-ins for your feedback. We conclude with a final review and the successful launch of your project.

Do you offer post-launch and offer support ?

Yes, absolutely. For our web development projects, we offer a range of ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website remains secure, updated, and performs optimally. We're committed to being your long-term creative partner.
